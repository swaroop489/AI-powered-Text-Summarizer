Artificial Intelligence (AI) enables machines to simulate human intelligence, learn from data, and perform complex tasks. It has transformed areas such as natural language processing, computer vision, healthcare, finance, transportation, and entertainment. AI applications include text summarization, image recognition, autonomous vehicles, predictive healthcare, fraud detection, and personalized recommendations. Ethical concerns like privacy, bias, and job displacement must be addressed. Future AI advancements, including explainable AI and AI-powered IoT, aim to augment human decision-making and drive innovation.
